LOSS FITNESS CENTER

What Makes Us Different?

Our unique payment plan forces our customer to get to the gym. This way, getting to the gym becomes way less of a hassle, and your goals become much easier to achieve. We want to make sure our customers are reaching their full potential, so our payment plans all involve incentives for getting to the gym every day.

Memberships

  1. The Original: This is the first plan we as a company offered. Full access to all facilities for only $20 a month plus $1 per day that you do not go to the gym.
  2. The Workhorse: This is a payment option for those slightly more committed to working out every day. Full access to all facilities for only $20 per month plus $2 per day that you do not go to the gym.
  3. The Gym Rat: This is a payment plans for those how already love going to the gym. Full access to all facilities for only $20 per month plus $5 for every day you do not go to the gym.

Our History

In 2015, our founder opened his first fitness center. After that, Loss Fitness exploded into a successful change of fitness centers all over the country. Through all of this, we as a company have kept only one goal in mind, to help our customers reach their own fitness goals.
