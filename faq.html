Frequently Asked Questions

What makes you different from your competitors?

We offer a friendly environment and a unique payment plan designed to help your goal be reached.

So what happens if I miss a day of lifting do to an emergency?

Nothing. You’ll be fined just like any other day you miss.

That seems kind of heartless…

It makes excuses more difficult. In a real emergency, losing a dollar will be the last thing on your mind. Otherwise, it is an effective way to keep our customers from skipping days at the gym.

How available are your personal trainers?

We keep at least three trainers in the gym at all times to help with workouts, and any of our staff is available by scheduling a workout.
